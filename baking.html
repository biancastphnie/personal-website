basic sugar cookie recipe


ingredients (for the cookie part):

  • 250 grams of all-purpose flour
  • 25 grams of corn flour
  • 75 grams of caster sugar (sub: granulated sugar)
  • 175 grams of unsalted butter
  • 2 tbsp of milk

ingredients (for the icing part):

  • 200 grams of icing sugar
  • 1 tbsp of lemon juice
  • 1 tbsp of warm water
  • optional: food colouring or sprinkles


directions:

  1. preheat the oven to 180Â°C
  2. line the baking tray with baking sheet
  3. sift the all-purpose flour, corn flour, and caster sugar into a bowl
  4. mix in the butter (in small chunks) with the dry ingredients, until the mixture looks like breadcrumbs
  5. stir in the milk
  6. dust the work surface and rolling pin with the all-purpose flour
  7. knead the dough on the work surface until it looks smooth
  8. roll out the dough to about half a centimeter
  9. use a cookie cutter to cut out the shape of the cookies and carefully place them on the baking sheet
  10. bake the cookies for about 10 to 12 minutes, or until they turn pale golden
  11. place them on a cooling rack to cool
  12. mix the icing sugar and the lemon juice together
  13. slowly add in a bit of water and mix until smooth
  14. separate some of the icing into a separate bowl if you want to add in some food colouring
  15. lastly, decorate the cooled cookies with the icing (and sprinkles if you want)
